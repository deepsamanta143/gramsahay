Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GramSahay ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our WhatsApp-based civic issue reporting service available at gramsahay.in.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with GramSahay through WhatsApp, we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name (as provided during the conversation)
  • Messages, issue descriptions, and queries you send to our service
  • Your village and area details (as selected during issue submission)
  • Issue type and status history
  • Usage data such as frequency and type of requests submitted

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Register and track your civic issue or complaint
  • Assign your request to the appropriate community coordinator
  • Send you status updates as your issue progresses
  • Enable coordinators and administrators to manage and resolve issues
  • Improve the GramSahay platform and its features
  • Generate anonymised reporting on community issues for administrative purposes

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With assigned community coordinators and administrators — solely for the purpose of resolving your reported issue
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

4. WhatsApp & Meta Platform Data

Our service operates through the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. Your interactions with GramSahay via WhatsApp are also subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it at whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain issue records and associated personal data for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain audit trails, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures — including JWT-secured APIs and role-based access controls — to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of the service at any time by messaging "STOP" on WhatsApp
  • Raise a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

8. Children's Privacy

GramSahay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our service evolves. We will notify users of significant changes through our WhatsApp service or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of GramSahay after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
