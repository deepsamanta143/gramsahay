The Problem

Rural communities have no easy way to raise civic issues

When a road breaks, water supply fails, or a light goes out — villagers have no structured way to report it, no reference number to track it, and no guarantee it will ever be addressed. GramSahay changes that.

What We Do

A platform built for rural India

GramSahay bridges the gap between citizens and community coordinators — using a channel everyone already has.

📢
Report
Citizens raise any civic issue from their village — instantly, in their own language, without downloading anything.
🔔
Notify
The right community coordinator is alerted immediately and given everything they need to take action.
✅
Resolve
Issues are tracked, updated, and closed — with citizens informed at every step until resolution.
