Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GramSahay ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our WhatsApp-based service available at gramsahay.in.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with GramSahay through WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages and queries you send to our service
  • Your approximate location (only if you share it voluntarily)
  • Language preference
  • Usage data such as frequency and type of queries

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our WhatsApp assistant service
  • Respond to your queries about market prices, government schemes, weather, and farming advice
  • Personalize responses based on your region and language preference
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the service
  • Send important service-related updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (subject to confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your consent

4. WhatsApp Data

Our service operates through the WhatsApp Business API. Your interactions are subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy in addition to ours. We encourage you to review WhatsApp's Privacy Policy at whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@gramsahay.in.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of our service at any time by messaging "STOP" on WhatsApp

8. Children's Privacy

GramSahay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our WhatsApp service or on our website.
